What is the probability of a game selling 1 million copies if it is made by X360 and from the Action genre, vs. the probability if it is made by WiiU and from the Strategy genre?¶

In [171]:
def prob(platform, genre):
    return len(df[(df['Genre'] == genre) & (df['Platform'] == platform)]) / len(df[df['Global_Sales'] >= 1.0])
